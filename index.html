Bond Lab is open source fixed income analytics. Bond Lab capabilities include the following:

  • Modified, effective, and key rate (partial duration)
  • Total Return Analysis of Mortgage Pass-Throughs (bond are coming soon)
  • Option Adjusted Spread analysis of Mortgage Pass-Throughs

Following the financial crisis structured finance industry called for open source waterfalls (payment rules) of mortgage- and asset-backed securities. Bond Lab Technologies, Inc. was founded to begin the process of building an open source fixed income analytics platform capable of supporting open source waterfalls.

BondLab as was used to the book Investing in Mortgage Backed and Asset Backed Securities, Financial Modeling with R and Open Source Analytics.
